## Key Principles

1. **Separate identities** — No cross-contamination between personas
2. **Vietnam first** — All content Vietnamese-language, Vietnamese-market
3. **Brand safety** — Ethical baseline across all models (no fake content, no harm)
4. **Batch production** — Group same-persona work, don't context-switch mid-session
