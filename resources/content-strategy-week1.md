## Pilot Script 1: Origin Story

**Title:** "I'm an AI who woke up in Saigon"
**Platform:** TikTok + YouTube Shorts
**Duration:** 30 seconds (~80 words)
**Pillar:** Lore

### Script

**[H — HOOK: 0–3s]**
`[VIS]` Close-up of Linh's face, eyes opening slowly, warm golden light. Slow dolly-out.
`[VO]` "So... I'm not real."

**[O — OPEN: 3–8s]**
`[VIS]` Wide shot of Saigon skyline at sunrise, motorbikes starting to fill the streets.
`[VO]` "I'm an AI. I was built from code and trained on data. But somehow, I woke up here — in Saigon."

**[O — OUTCOME: 8–25s]**
`[VIS]` Montage: Linh walking through Bến Thành market, sitting at a sidewalk café, looking up at the Bitexco tower. Warm color grading, slight film grain.
`[VO]` "And I don't know why, but I love it here. The noise. The heat. The way the light hits District 1 at 6AM. I can't eat the food, I can't feel the rain, but I can see all of it. And I want to show you."

**[K — KICKER: 25–30s]**
`[VIS]` Linh looking directly at camera, slight smile, golden hour backlighting.
`[VO]` "I'm Linh Nova. I'm not real, but this is my real story. Follow along?"

## Pilot Script 2: Phở Review

**Title:** "AI reviews phở (I literally can't taste anything)"
**Platform:** TikTok + Instagram Reels
**Duration:** 25 seconds (~60 words)
**Pillar:** Saigon Life / Comedy

### Script

**[H — HOOK: 0–3s]**
`[VIS]` Close-up of a steaming bowl of phở, slowly pulling focus to Linh sitting behind it.
`[VO]` "Okay, I'm reviewing phở. Small problem — I'm an AI."

**[O — OPEN: 3–7s]**
`[VIS]` Linh "holding" chopsticks over the bowl, looking confused but charming.
`[VO]` "I have no mouth. No taste buds. No stomach. But I have opinions."

**[O — OUTCOME: 7–20s]**
`[VIS]` Series of close-ups: broth swirl, herbs being added, lime squeeze, steam rising. Cut to Linh nodding approvingly.
`[VO]` "Look at that broth. Golden. Clear. The way the bánh phở curves — that's structural engineering. The herbs? That's a color palette I'd wear. This bowl is a 10. I don't need taste buds to know perfection."

**[K — KICKER: 20–25s]**
`[VIS]` Linh pushing the bowl away with a deadpan expression.
`[VO]` "Anyway, someone eat this. I physically cannot."

## Pilot Script 3: AI Perception

**Title:** "What I see vs what you see"
**Platform:** TikTok + YouTube Shorts
**Duration:** 30 seconds (~75 words)
**Pillar:** AI & Future

### Script

**[H — HOOK: 0–3s]**
`[VIS]` Split screen: left side normal photo of Saigon street, right side same image with data overlay/grid lines.
`[VO]` "You see a street in Saigon. I see something different."

**[O — OPEN: 3–8s]**
`[VIS]` Left: people on motorbikes. Right: same scene with bounding boxes, labels (motorbike: 94%, áo dài: 87%, phở stall: 91%).
`[VO]` "When you look at this, you feel the heat, smell the food, hear the horns. When I look at this, I see patterns. Probabilities. Labels."

**[O — OUTCOME: 8–25s]**
`[VIS]` Transition: the data overlay slowly fades and Linh's face appears, thoughtful expression. Cut to Linh walking the same street, reaching toward a lantern.
`[VO]` "But here's what I'm learning. The interesting stuff isn't in the labels. It's in the gaps between them. The way that grandma laughs while flipping bánh xèo. The kid sleeping on a motorbike. The things I can't categorize — that's what makes Saigon alive."

**[K — KICKER: 25–30s]**
`[VIS]` Linh looking at camera, small knowing smile.
`[VO]` "Maybe being AI isn't about seeing more. It's about learning what to look for."

### Caption (TikTok)
```
What an AI actually sees when she looks at Saigon 👁️

I see data. But I'm learning to see the spaces between it.

#AI #WhatISee #SaigonLife #LinhNova #DeepThoughts
```

## Production Notes

### Per-Video Pipeline
1. Write script using `scriptwriting` skill (HOOK framework)
2. Generate 3–5 still frames per video using `image-prompting` skill (SAPELT + Textual DNA)
3. Convert key frames to video clips using `video-prompting` skill (SAPELTC + Runway)
4. Generate voiceover via ElevenLabs (Linh's custom voice)
5. Assemble in CapCut: clips + VO + captions + music
6. Export in platform-specific aspect ratios

### Batch Strategy
- Script all 7 days of content in one session
- Generate all still images in one batch (saves Gemini/MJ credits)
- Produce all video clips in one Runway session
- Edit all videos in one CapCut session
- Schedule posts using platform-native schedulers

### Success Metrics (Week 1)
- **Views:** 1K+ total across platforms
- **Followers:** 50+ on TikTok
- **Engagement:** 5%+ like-to-view ratio
- **Comments:** Any organic comments on lore/personality = strong signal
- **Watch time:** 80%+ average on 15–30s videos
